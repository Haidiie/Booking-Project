OUR ROOMS AND SUITES

SINGLE ROOM

Single Room
There's no such thing as a standard room at our Hotel, and our Single Rooms, Are no exception. Each room in this category is individually decorated, providing the perfect retreat for the solo traveller.

JUNIOR SUITE

Junior Suite
Our Junior Suites boast elegant furnishings, a lavish bathroom and distinctive sitting area for the ultimate feeling of space and comfort.

SUPERIOR SUITE

Superior Suite
Indulge in a little more space in a Superior Suite, comprising a separate bedroom and living room. Wake up to a lazy morning in a sumptuous king-sized bed, or spend time relaxing or working in the sitting room.

EXECUTIVE SUITE

Executive Suite
Enjoy even more of the suite life in an expansive Executive Suite. Boasting sweeping waterfront views over the city, with a magnificent interior to match.

ROOFTOP SUITE

The Rooftop Suite. promises an unforgettable experience with sweeping views of the city. Revel in beautiful details such as the plush velvet chairs on geometric patterned rugs.

TERRACE SUITE

ROYAL SUITE

Royal Suite
In this lavish suite the royal splendour is more pronounced than usual. Simply open the doors to the French balcony for unparalleled views of the Royal Palace just across the water.

DELUXE ROOM

Deluxe Room
